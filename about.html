About Me

I have a passion for escaping into nature, connecting with my roots, and capturing the true essence of mood and emotion through imagery. I seek to find the spark, or magic, of what lies in front of me- be it the joy behind someone's smile, or the glistening of fresh dew on a tree. Photography is my art of choice because of the ability to truly cease time, creating tangible memories. I wish to convey both realism and beauty through my photographic work.
